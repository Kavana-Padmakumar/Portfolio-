We rebuilt the Stellar Hack deck more times than I'd like to admit, mostly rejecting our own polish. The version that finally worked was the plainest one — white space, one font, a hook line per slide. Restraint was the whole lesson.

MAR 2026#hardware

LakeGuard's real bottleneck wasn't the model

The LSTM was never the hard part — getting a floating ESP32 rig to survive a lake for a week without drifting off-position was. Field testing taught me more about system design than any dataset did.

FEB 2026#notes-to-self

Docker + FastAPI, the boring parts

Writing this down so future-me stops relearning it: pin your base image, health-check the container before wiring it into anything else, and never let a hackathon demo depend on a cold start.
